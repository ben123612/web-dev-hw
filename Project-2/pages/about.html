Why A recipe for this Sandwhich?

Seven years ago, while living in Wisconsin, one of my friends decided to move to Colorado with his fiance. Before they left, they wanted to do something fun with a group of friends. They decided to go to northern Wisconsin and do a rafting trip! We figured out all the logistics, which included each person being responsible for bringing one lunch for everyone. After all our gear was packed, we hit the road for some adventure! After several tiring days, with not enough time on the river and too much time around a fire swapping drunken stories, it was our last day and my friends turn to provide lunch. He decided to make the Peanut Butter, Nutella, banana, and honey sandwhich! It was one of the best adventure sandwhiches i'd ever had!

Why do I continue to make this sandwhich?!

Whether I am going out for a mountain bike ride, hike or for some nordic skiing, this sandwhich is still my go to. Why? Well, it's delicious, it provides lots of energy and it reminds me of my friends! I definitely suggest you give it a try!
