Perfect Sando

If you're looking for the perfect sandwhich to take on your next adventure, look no further!

Too few pople understand a really GOOD sandwhich.

-James Beard

Peanut butter, banana, honey, and nutella sandwhich!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 banana -Make sure it's ripe!
  • Honey -If possible, buy local!
  • Nutella -Or another hazelnut cocoa spread!
  • Peanut butter -Crunchy is my favorite, but it's up to you!
  • Bread -Whole grain, but any bread works!
  • Other supplies
    1. Knife
    2. Tin-foil

How to make this delicious sandwhich!

Step-1

 -Slice your bread to desired thickness. If bread is already cut, take two slices and get ready for step-2.

Step-2

 -Spread desired amount of Peanut Butter on one side of the bread with knife.

Step-3

 -Spread desired amount of nutella on the other piece of bread with knife.

Step-4

 -Cut the banana, with knife, into thing slices and layer on one side of sandwhich.

Step-5

 - Put honey on sandwhich and then put two slices of bread together.
