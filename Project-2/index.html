Peanut butter, banana, honey, and nutella sandwhich!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 banana -You can use a whole Banana if you'd like, but it can get messy!
  • Honey -Apply desired amount!
  • Nutella -Again, apply as much as you'd like!
  • Peanut butter -Crunchy is better! Apply same amount of Peanut butter as nutella.
  • Bread -Use preferred bread, untoasted for best results.
  • Other supplies
    1. Knife
    2. Tin-foil
